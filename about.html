About us

Digitize Health Care is a healthcare platform that connects the user to top notch doctors online. And we also included online medicine delivery and blood bank system into this application.Through this application our aim is to to have a society wherein there is no shortage of blood and efficient doctor services. Digitize Health Care helps you find online medical services and solutions to enable you to take better care of yourself.Our system is a standalone system that can be installed on doctors and patients android phone to be used for further login, doctor understand patient related difficulties such that patient get replied as possible early and he save his precious life in few second. our project mainly concentrated on government hospital. It creates a convenient and easy to use application for the users
